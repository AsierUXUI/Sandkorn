About Sandkorn
🌾
Boycotting works.
It always has.
From the Montgomery Bus Boycott to the anti-apartheid movement, organised consumer withdrawal has changed the world. Sandkorn is a small tool to make that easier today.
Why collective action works
💶
Every euro is a vote
Companies respond to revenue loss faster than to any petition. Your subscription is their planning assumption. Cancelling it breaks that assumption.
📊
MAU numbers move markets
Meta's stock price is tied to Monthly Active Users. When 4,500 people leave one city, it shows in earnings calls. Investors notice. Strategy changes.
📰
Media follows momentum
Organised boycotts above a threshold get press coverage, reaching people who never heard of Sandkorn.
Boycotting is not enough on its own
Consumer withdrawal removes funding. But systemic change also needs political pressure, community alternatives, and people organising in physical space. That is why Sandkorn includes events, local alternatives, and community infrastructure — not just deletion guides.
The goal is not a pure conscience. The goal is leverage. Sandkorn is one tool in a larger set.
How to start
1
Pick one company. Not ten. Start where you have real alternatives already.
2
Use the deletion guide. We walk you through every step including the GDPR route.
3
Tell two people. Collective action is not possible alone. The only lever is scale.
4
Show up to something. Events near you happen regularly. Showing up matters.
